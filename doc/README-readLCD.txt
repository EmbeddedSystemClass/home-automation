To figure out the crop geometry:
 - Open the downloaded picture to your desktop and open it with Gimp.
 - Use 'Rectangle Select' tool and select your LCD display. Now you have the geometry. I fill in my values below so you know what is what
  - Position: 1357, 324
  - Size: 74, 105
 - See gimp_screenshot1.png for a visual explanation of the numbers above.

To figure out text color
 - Open the picture in Gimp as above
 - Choose color picker (shortcut button o) and click on the text that should be read with OCR
 - Double click on the color chooser square. Your color is the number in the 'HTML notation' field.
